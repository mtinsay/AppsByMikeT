Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Simple Interval Timer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: Simple Interval Timer stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Your workout data remains on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Simple Interval Timer, you may provide the following information:

  • Workout Data: Workout timers, intervals, work/rest durations, and round configurations that you create
  • Workout History: Records of completed workouts including timestamps and duration data
  • Audio Preferences: Your selected chimes and countdown cues
  • App Settings: Your preferences for app configuration

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app with ads enabled, the following information may be collected by third-party ad providers:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (such as GAID on Android or IDFA on iOS) for ad personalization and analytics
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with advertisements
  • Location Data: General location information (country-level) for ad delivery and compliance with regional regulations

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Data Usage

All locally stored information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app:

  • Creating and managing your workout routines
  • Tracking your workout history and progress
  • Personalizing your audio experience
  • Remembering your app preferences

2.2 Ad-Related Data Usage

When ads are enabled, third-party ad providers (Google AdMob) may use collected information to:

  • Deliver personalized advertisements based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Comply with legal requirements and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All workout data and preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite database and shared preferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

3.3 Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete it manually through the app's "Delete All Data" feature
  • Uninstall the application
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use information about you to provide ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information:

  • Google Ads Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, we use Google UMP to obtain consent for personalized advertising. This helps us comply with GDPR requirements.

5. Your Rights

5.1 Data Access and Portability

You can export all your workout data at any time through the Data Management feature in the app settings.

5.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to delete all your data. You can do this through:

  • In-App Deletion: Go to Settings → Delete All Data
  • Device Settings: Clear app data through your device's application settings

5.3 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads through:

  • The Privacy & Ads section in our app settings
  • Your device's advertising settings (Android: Google Settings → Ads, iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising)
  • Industry opt-out tools: aboutads.info

6. Children's Privacy

Simple Interval Timer is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

If you access the app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying a notice in the app

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
